The real magic behind modern AI models like ChatGPT lies in a powerful concept called the Transformer architecture, particularly the "Attention mechanism." While most people focus on the massive size of these models—often containing billions of parameters—the true secret is how they pay attention to different parts of a sentence. The Attention mechanism enables the model to determine which words are most important for understanding the overall meaning, allowing it to focus more on those key elements. For instance, in the sentence “Sami went to the market because he forgot his wallet,” the model understands that "he" refers to Sami, not the market—thanks to attention.
