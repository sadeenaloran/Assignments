Weather Overview

Weather refers to the atmospheric conditions in a specific place at a given time. It includes factors like temperature, humidity, wind speed, precipitation, and air pressure.

Weather Changes

Weather can change rapidly, ranging from sunny and warm to rainy, snowy, or stormy. These changes are influenced by geographic location, seasons, and climate patterns.

Weather Forecasting

Meteorologists use tools like satellites and weather models to predict weather conditions. Accurate forecasts help people prepare for daily activities and extreme events such as hurricanes or heatwaves.
